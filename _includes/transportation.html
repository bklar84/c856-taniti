Transportation

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. Taniti is in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the island within the next few years.

Public Transportation

Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily.

Private Bus

Serves the rest of the island outside of Taniti City.

Taxi

Available in Taniti City.

Car Rental

Can be rented from a local agency near the airport.

Other

Bikes and helmet (required by law) can be rented from various vendors around the island. The flat landscape of Taniti City also makes is a breeze to explore by foot.
