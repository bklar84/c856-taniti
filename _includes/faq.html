Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your top questions.

Language

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents.

Currency

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.

Crime

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti, but as tourism increases, there are more reports of imgkpocketing and other petty crimes.

Medical

There is one hospital (777 Side Street, Taniti City) and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

Electricity

ower outlets are 120 volts (same as in the United States)

Alcohol

The drinking age on Taniti is 18, but not strictly enforced. Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m.

Holidays

Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays, and many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.
